REBATE MANAGEMENT CONFIGURATION

Tiered Rebate Structure (Annual Volume):
- 0% for $0-$25K
- 2% for $25K-$75K
- 3% for $75K-$150K
- 4% for $150K+

Accrual Basis:
- At Invoice: Calculate rebate, post to Rebate Liability
- At Claim: Finance reviews, generates Credit Memo
- Reduces Customer AR Balance

Claim Processing:
- Customer files claim quarterly/annually
- Finance approves/rejects in MCRT
- Approved: Credit Memo reduces AR

Reporting:
- Rebate Accrual Report: Lists liabilities by customer
- Aging Analysis: Unclaimed rebates > 12 months
